UG-Placement Training


The Department of Networking and Communications (NWC) organized a series of impactful placement training sessions in October 2024 to prepare students for upcoming recruitment drives.


Highlights of the events include:

Placement Mock Test Series 01 – Technical Aptitude Tests 04 & 05: Held on October 4 and 19, these tests, coordinated by Dr. M. Maranco, Dr. N. Deepa, Dr. Mursal Hamdani, and Dr. R. Logeshwari, assessed technical skills and aptitude.

Placement Mock Interviews (Sessions 15–23): Aimed at refining students’ interview readiness, sessions were conducted on multiple dates, coordinated by Dr. M. Maranco and Dr. M. Nivedhitha.

Programming the Fun Way – Session 2: On October 8, this engaging session, led by Dr. M. Maranco, Dr. Mursal Hamdani, and Dr. N. Deepa, provided an innovative approach to programming.

These initiatives were met with enthusiastic participation and students’ technical, significantly enhanced problem-solving, and interview preparation skills. The department remains committed to empowering students for successful placement outcomes.

The placement training program conducted during October, November, and December effectively prepared students for the upcoming recruitment season. By integrating technical aptitude, general aptitude, industry interaction, and interview practice, the program offered a well-rounded approach that enhanced students’ skills and provided valuable exposure to real world placement scenarios.

The enthusiastic participation of students, coupled with positive feedback on the training sessions, underscores the program’s impact. We are confident that these initiatives will substantially boost students’ employability and performance in the forthcoming campus recruitment drives.
