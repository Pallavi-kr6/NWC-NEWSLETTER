My Journey as an SRM Alumnus

Dear SRM Community,

I am honored to share my experiences as an alumnus of SRM Institute of Science and Technology, where I completed my B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering with a specialization in Information Technology from 2019 to 2023.

Reflecting on my time at SRM, I am grateful for the vibrant platform it provided for education and exploration. The diverse range of courses and activities offered a strong foundation, but one of the most important lessons I learned is that education is a personal journey.

While SRM sets the stage, the responsibility to learn and grow rests with everyone. Taking the initiative to delve deeper into subjects, seek out additional resources, and pursue personal projects is key to truly mastering any field.

To my juniors, here are a few insights I’d like to share:

  • 1. Take Charge of Your Learning: Use the resources available at SRM as a starting point, but always be proactive in seeking out knowledge beyond the curriculum.
  • 2. Explore and Experiment: Don’t hesitate to explore different areas within your field. This will help you identify your interests and build a versatile skill set.
  • 3. Build a Strong Network: Technical skills are crucial, but networking with peers, faculty, and industry professionals opens doors to new opportunities.
  • 4. Stay Curious and Adaptable: The tech industry is always evolving. Cultivate a mindset of curiosity and adaptability to stay ahead in your career.
  • 5. Learn from Every Experience: Successes and setbacks alike are valuable learning opportunities. Embrace them as part of your growth journey.

I look forward to seeing how current and future students continue to innovate and excel. Remember, while SRM provides the platform, your journey of learning and success is uniquely yours.
