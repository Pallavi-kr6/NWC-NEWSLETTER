Specialized Lab: Embedded AI Lab

The Embedded AI Lab, established in collaboration with NVIDIA GTC in February 2024, serves as a research hub dedicated to advancing artificial intelligence technologies. Fully sponsored by NVIDIA, the lab focuses on a wide spectrum of AI applications, including computer vision, natural language processing, robotics, and reinforcement learning. The lab’s objective is to foster cutting-edge research, innovation, and development in AI to address real-world challenges.

Ongoing Projects:

1. JetWifiScan: Wireless Network Scanning Application using NVIDIA Jetson Orin
2. Ear Biometric System
3. IoT-Based Animal Detection and Alert System
4. Student Attendance Monitoring System
5. Garbage Detection in Hallways and Universities
6. Human Safety Detection at Construction Sites
7. Person Identification System for Departments

Memorandum of Understanding (MoU)

In the specialization of Information Technology (IT) the MoUs were signed with Ajna Labs Pvt. Ltd. (2021), VEI Technologies (2021), and ExcelR Solutions (2022). The Ajna Labs MoU, spanning five years, facilitates student internships, joint research projects, and placements in the Computer Vision domain. The VEI Technologies MoU (2021-2026) introduced a four-week internship program in 2021, engaging 25 students. Meanwhile, the ExcelR Solutions MoU (2022 2027) conducted a 40-hour training program in 2022, benefiting 135 students. These collaborations focus on practical training, industry exposure, and research initiatives, enhancing students’ skills and employability in the IT sector

India Mobile Congress 2024 – Showcasing Innovation in Smart Cities


Date : October 15–18, 2024
Venue : Pragati Maidan, New Delhi
Presenter : Dr. M. Thenmozhi

The 8th Edition of the India Mobile Congress (IMC) served as a global platform for technological advancements in 5G, AI, and smart solutions. Dr. M. Thenmozhi proudly represented the department by showcasing a Smart Parking System, an IoT-powered solution designed to optimize urban mobility.
Smart Parking System Features:
AI-based real-time space detection and allocation.
IoT sensors for automated monitoring and reduced congestion.
Mobile app integration for seamless user access.

Impact:

The innovation attracted industry interest, paving the way for potential collaborations in smart infrastructure development

NLP Workshop – Advancing AI in Language Processing

Date: December 2–9, 2024
Venue: Panimalar Engineering College
Speaker: Dr. Meenakshi K
As part of the Faculty Development Program (FDP) on “NLP Using Pre-trained Language Models and Transfer Learning,” Dr. K.Meenakshi delivered an insightful seminar on “Basic Introduction to NLP and Hands-on Training.”
Key Takeaways: Introduction to Natural Language Processing (NLP) concepts. Hands-on training with pre-trained models and transfer learning. Applications of NLP in speech recognition, sentiment analysis, and chatbots. Outcome: Faculty members gained practical expertise in NLP model implementation, enabling them to integrate AI-driven solutions into academic research and teaching.
