Spotlight on Innovation

FUNDED RESEARCH PROJECTS

October - December 2024

We are delighted to highlight the innovative projects funded under the Selective Excellence Research Initiative (SERI) - 2024, which showcase the groundbreaking contributions of our faculty members in healthcare and wearable technologies.

Medic Reminder: A Mobile App for Maintaining Your Health Regimen

Principal Investigator: Dr. Vaishnavi Moorthy

Funding Agency: SERI (Selective Excellence Research Initiative – 2024)

Project Highlights: This project aims to develop Medic Food - A Drug-Food Interaction Mobile Application (Phase 2), a user-friendly app designed to help individuals manage their health regimen effectively. The application focuses on drug-food interactions to improve health outcomes.

Funding Amount: ₹1,15,000/-

Smart Wearable Fog Forecaster for De-Fog Unit in Parkinson’s Disease

Principal Investigator: Dr. Banu Priya

Funding Agency: SERI (Selective Excellence Research Initiative – 2024)

Project Highlights: This project explores cutting-edge wearable technology designed to address Freezing of Gait (FoG) in Parkinson’s Disease patients. The innovative “De-Fog Unit” aims to forecast and mitigate FoG episodes, improving mobility and quality of life.

Funding Amount: ₹2,15,000/-

These projects underline our institution’s commitment to advancing research in healthcare technology and fostering a culture of innovation.
